Signature Philosophy

We believe that beauty is not a surface-level pursuit, but an intimate dialogue between the body and the spirit. Our rituals are meticulously designed to act as a catalyst for this connection.

Our Founder

Mugdha Singh

Founded in 2025, The Euphoric Soul is the culmination of Mugdha Singh’s lifelong obsession with the science of radiance. She spent years immersing herself in advanced skincare philosophies in **Seoul, South Korea**.

Her vision was to bring the surgical precision of Korean "Glass Skin" protocols and the holistic depth of Eastern scalp rituals to Mumbai, creating a destination for the truly intentional.
